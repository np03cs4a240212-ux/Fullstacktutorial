Chocolate Chip Cookies

Today , we're gonna share a classic homemade cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Scoop small portions onto a baking tray.
  3. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes.
